## The short version

- There are **no accounts** — you don't sign in, and we don't ask who you are.
- Your **PDFs stay on your device**. We never upload your files.
- The only thing we receive is the **text you select** when you ask for an explanation.
- We run **no analytics, no ads, and no third-party trackers**.
- We **don't sell or share** your data, and we don't use it to build a profile of you.

## What we collect

**The text you select for an explanation.** When you long-press a word, swipe a
line, or sweep a passage, that selected text — and the small amount of
surrounding context needed to explain it accurately — is sent to our servers so
we can generate a response.

**Basic technical logs.** When your app contacts our backend, our servers record
standard request information (such as a timestamp and error details) needed to
operate and debug the service.

That's it. We do **not** collect your name, email, contacts, location,
advertising identifiers, the contents of your documents, or a history of what
you read.

## How explanations are generated

To produce an explanation, we send your selected text to **Google's Gemini API**.
Google processes that text to generate the response, returns it to us, and we
relay it to your app. We don't send Google your identity — we don't have it.

Your selected text is handled by Google under its API terms. We use the Gemini
API in a paid configuration under which Google does **not** use the content we
send to train its models.

You can read Google's privacy practices at
[policies.google.com/privacy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).

## Your choices

Because there are no accounts and we don't store your selections, there's usually
nothing for us to look up, export, or delete on request. If you believe we hold
information about you and want it removed, email us and we'll help.

## Cookies

The app doesn't use cookies. This website doesn't set cookies or use any tracking
scripts either — there's no banner because there's nothing to consent to.

## Children

{SITE.product} isn't directed to children under 13, and we don't knowingly
collect information from them. If you believe a child has provided us
information, contact us and we'll delete it.

## Security

Traffic between the app and our servers is encrypted in transit (HTTPS/TLS). No
method of transmission is perfectly secure, but we take reasonable measures to
protect the limited data we handle.

## Changes to this policy

If we change this policy, we'll update the "Last updated" date above. For
significant changes, we'll do our best to give clear notice in the app or here.
